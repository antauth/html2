### Topic A - Planning a Web site

- **Objective** What is the goal? SMART - Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Revelant, Time-based
- **Content** What content will be on the site?
- **Audience** What are the interests, ages, experiences, background, and expectations of your audience? (Impacts design)
- **Site structure** Determine early, changing could be very hard

#### Analyzing the site's purpose and audience

- Client goals
- Plan to achieve goals
- Demographics of target audience
- Engagement
- User's needs
- Browser considerations
- Internet access speed

### Design considerations

- Retain users by designing pages that make it easy for them to find info
- Intuitive content and navigation structure
- Thoughtful graphics, color, typography

#### Factors

- **Navigation:** consistent on every page, reflect site structure
- **Fonts:** optimal readability, appropriate for audience
- **Page length:** manaageable chunks
- **Color and contrast:** easy to read against background color, accessible
- **Load time:** Slow load times deter visitors
- **Typography:** Keep body text simple. Adjust font styles to improve readability.

**Keep most important information "above the fold"**
