## Topic D: Basic SEO
Optimize your content for Search Engine Results Pages (SERPs)

### Metadata content

Responsible for...

* presentation of document
* behavior of document
* establishes relationships with other sources (e.g. `<script>`,
`<link>`, `<style>`, `<meta>`)
* belongs in `<head>` section of document

### Page titles

* Unique for every page
* Concisely describe page content
* Use title casing
* Avoid generic page titles
* Incorporate branding (e.g. put company name in each title)
* Do not insert a list of keywords

### Page descriptions

* Not included in search engine ranking algorithms
* May be displayed as snippets in SERPs and on social media

`<meta name="description"
        content="This is where your page description goes." />`

#### Guidelines

* 155 characters or less
* Proper grammar and punctation
* People first in mind
* Avoid repeating same description on multiple pages
* When applicable, summarize most important data
* When applicable, include a call to action
* Make sure your descriptions are relevant to page content
* Avoid generic claims
